Cancellation Policy

Welcome to QrSay! We're committed to providing you with a seamless and satisfying experience. Our cancellation policy is designed to ensure fairness and clarity for both our users and restaurant partners. Please read the following policy carefully before placing an order.

Order Cancellation by Users:

  • Cancellation Window: Users can cancel their orders without any charge within 5 minutes of placing the order.
  • Cancellation Fees: After the initial cancellation window has passed, a cancellation fee of 5% of the order value will be charged to the user.
  • Refund Process: If the cancellation falls within the acceptable window, users will receive a full refund to their original payment method. If a cancellation fee applies, the refund amount will be the order amount minus the cancellation fee.
  • Refund Timeline: The refunded amount will typically be processed within 5 business days, depending on the payment method and bank processing times.

Order Cancellation by Restaurants:

  • Restaurant Request: In some cases, a restaurant partner might request to cancel an order due to unavailability of items or unforeseen circumstances. In such cases, the restaurant will inform us, and we will notify the user promptly.
  • User Communication: Users will be informed of the cancellation via email, SMS, or app notification, along with the reason provided by the restaurant.
  • Refund Process: If the order is canceled by the restaurant before preparation or delivery has begun, users will receive a full refund.

Exceptions:

  • Quality Issues: If the delivered food is of substandard quality or does not meet the expected standards, users can request a refund or replacement within 1 hours of delivery.
  • Late Deliveries: If an order is delivered after the estimated delivery time by more than 40 minutes, users can request a refund for the delivery charges.
  • Force Majeure: In cases of unforeseen events such as natural disasters, strikes, or government regulations that lead to order cancellations, neither users nor restaurants will be held liable.

Steps for Order Cancellation:

  1. Log in to your account on Your Website Name.
  2. Go to the "Order History" section.
  3. Find the order you wish to cancel and click on "Cancel Order."
  4. Follow the prompts to complete the cancellation process.

Modification and Amendments:

We reserve the right to modify or amend this cancellation policy at any time to ensure its relevance and effectiveness. Any changes will be communicated to users through our website, app, or other appropriate means. It's advisable to review the cancellation policy periodically to stay informed about any updates.

Responsibilities of Users:

  • Accurate Information: Users are responsible for providing accurate and up-to-date information while placing orders. Any discrepancies in contact details or delivery addresses that lead to order cancellations or delays will be the user's responsibility.
  • Timely Response: In cases where we need to contact users regarding their orders or cancellations, a prompt response is appreciated to ensure smooth communication.

Responsibilities of Restaurants:

  • Item Availability: Restaurants are responsible for updating their menu items' availability in real-time on our platform. Failure to do so might lead to cancellations if items are unavailable after an order is placed.
  • Order Accuracy: Restaurants are expected to prepare and deliver orders according to the specifications provided by users. Inaccurate orders or mismatched items are subject to user complaints and potential cancellations.
